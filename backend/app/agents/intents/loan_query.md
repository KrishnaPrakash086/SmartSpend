# Intent: loan_query

## Examples

- what are my loans
- my EMIs
- loan details
- show EMI schedule
- mortgage info
- how much do I owe
- loan balance
- list my loans
- what loans do I have
- show all EMIs

loan, loans, emi, emis, mortgage, tenure, owe, debt, installment
