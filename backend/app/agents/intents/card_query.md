# Intent: card_query

## Description
User wants to VIEW or CHECK existing credit/debit card information.

## Examples
- show my credit cards
- what are my cards
- card details
- show card balance
- credit card info
- my card limits
- display cards
- list my cards
- what cards do I have
- card utilization
- how much credit do I have

## Keywords
credit card, debit card, cards, card details, card balance, card limit, card info, show cards, list cards, my cards
